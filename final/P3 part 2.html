about P3

Pipeline With a Purpose Project aims to support African Americans males ages 16-24 years old who are fathers and/or living in economically disasvanteged communities to identify career paths, and actively pursue and achieve their career goals.

Core Values

Connection: Programs' success is tied to the youth's ability to connect with individuals. This provied program participants with real-life examples of what is possible. Calibration: Program participants learn the skills of taking stock of their lives from both a professional and personal perspective. Once the particpant can determine thier starting point, they can map a plan towards their desired outcomes.

Outcomes

This program model will enable-particpants to make significant strides along the career pathway specteum by 1. Building skills to obtain livable wages. 2. Utilize resouces and the support from P3 to help young people achieve their goals.3. Obtaining a sustainable livable wage, and better providing for themselves, their families, and their community.
