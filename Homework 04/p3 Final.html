About P3

Pipeline With a Purpose Project aims to support African Americans males ages 16-24 years old who are fathers and/or living in economically disasvanteged communities to identify career paths, and actively pursue and achieve their career goals. p3 is a hybrid program model that provides workface development skills training along with career and life coaching.

Core Values

Connection: Programs' success is tied to the youth's ability to connect with individuals. This provied program participants with real-life examples of what is possible. Calibration: Program participants learn the skills of taking stock of their lives from both a professional and personal perspective. Once the particpant can determine thier starting point, they can map a plan towards their desired outcomes.
